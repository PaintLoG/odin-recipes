Italian Lasagne

Tough but worth

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.
You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

You'll need:

  • 500 g ground meat (beef or mixed)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 800 g tomato passata
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 200 g lasagna noodles
  • 500 ml milk
  • 50 g butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 150 g grated cheese (e.g., mozzarella, parmesan)
  • Oregano, basil, salt, pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Let's do it:

  1. Fry the onion and garlic in olive oil, add the meat and cook until browned.
  2. Add the tomato passata, tomato paste, spices, and simmer over low heat for 15 minutes.
  3. Prepare the béchamel sauce: melt the butter, add the flour, then gradually pour in the milk, stirring until smooth.
  4. Spread a little meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish and cover with lasagna noodles.
  5. Layer: meat sauce, béchamel, noodles — and repeat until all ingredients are used. Top with grated cheese.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (356°F) for about 40 minutes.
  7. Let rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
